What is
Muscle Testing?

Your body has its own intelligence. Muscle testing is a gentle way of asking the body yes/no questions — not the thinking mind, but the deeper subconscious. A subtle change in muscle response reveals the answer. It is painless, non-invasive, and surprisingly accurate.
